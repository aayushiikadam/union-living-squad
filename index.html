Questions

Everything you need to know.

Still have questions? Email us at info@unionliving.in or WhatsApp +91 91379 15406.

Who can join the ambassador program?
+
Any student in a city where Union Living operates — Mumbai, Pune, or Gurugram. No sales experience or investment required.
When does my first payment arrive?
+
The same day your friend pays their security deposit. 50% of your reward is transferred to your UPI immediately — no waiting at all.
What triggers the second payment?
+
7 days after your friend's confirmed move-in, once all payments are cleared. We send a WhatsApp confirmation the moment it's done.
Is there a cap on how many I can refer?
+
No limit at all. Every successful move-in is paid, and hitting milestones unlocks higher tiers and higher pay per referral automatically.
What counts as a successful referral?
+
Your friend must book through your unique link and complete their move-in. A visit or enquiry alone does not trigger a payout.
How do I track my referrals?
+
Through your personal ambassador dashboard — available once you're onboarded. See every click, lead, and payout in real time.
